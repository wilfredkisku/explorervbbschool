Academics

A clear academic page with structure, stages and learning priorities.

This page is inspired by product and feature-card layouts so parents can understand the school’s academic journey at a glance.

Stage 1
Pre-Primary

Play-based readiness in language, numbers, movement, classroom confidence and social interaction.

Stage 2
Primary

Foundational language, mathematics, EVS, reading habits, hands-on learning and reflective routines.

Stage 3
Middle School

Subject depth, science inquiry, mathematics practice, communication and project-based exploration.

Stage 4
Senior Years

Focused preparation, academic discipline, mentoring and future-ready habits for learners.

Curriculum priorities

Essential learning pillars

Language & expression

Reading fluency, confident speaking, writing clarity and regular exposure to communication-rich activities.

Mathematics & logic

Conceptual understanding, numerical confidence, routine practice and problem solving with guidance.

Science & inquiry

Observation, questioning, simple investigations and curiosity-driven learning experiences.

Social understanding

Awareness of the world, culture, environment, citizenship and value-based classroom discussions.

Digital readiness

Basic technology familiarity, responsible use and comfort with modern learning tools where appropriate.

Public speaking

Assemblies, presentations and classroom opportunities that help students express ideas with confidence.
