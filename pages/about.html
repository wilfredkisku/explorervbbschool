About the school

About Explorer & V.B.B. School

A values-led school environment focused on the physical, mental and spiritual development of every learner.

A school built around guided growth

Education that nurtures body, mind and spirit

The school emphasizes that the education of a child shapes the future, and that teachers and parents both play a meaningful role in guiding each step of a learner’s journey.

Its public message presents education as a process that nurtures body, mind and spirit, while helping children develop character, understanding and readiness for a changing world.

Child-centered educational approach
Supportive prepared environment
Learning guided by curiosity
Enriched education for excellence
Principal's message

The principal’s message welcomes students, teachers, parents and well-wishers into a school that sees education as all-round development.

Education is presented as a lifelong journey that continues both at school and at home, supported through partnership between educators and families.

Vision & mission

What the school stands for

The official pages repeatedly emphasize enriched learning, individual potential and a culture that inspires bright futures.

  • • Develop the unique abilities and potential of every child.
  • • Encourage creativity, determination, ambition and brighter futures.
  • • Build a child-centered culture based on observation, curiosity and support.
